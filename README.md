# ShopMart ECommerce-AI-ChatBot
ShopMart is a responsive and modern E-Commerce platform inspired by leading online shopping sites like Amazon and Flipkart. It is designed to deliver a smooth shopping experience while integrating an intelligent AI-powered Assistant that helps customers with queries in real time.  Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

## ShopMart 

## 🛍️ About ShopMart

Welcome to ShopMart – your one-stop online shopping destination!

At ShopMart, we bring together quality products, trusted brands, and seamless shopping all in one place. Whether you’re looking for electronics, fashion, groceries, books, cosmetics, or daily essentials, we’ve got you covered.

Our mission is simple: to make shopping easy, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. With a user-friendly platform, secure payment options, and fast delivery services, ShopMart ensures that your shopping journey is smooth from browsing to checkout.

✨ Why Shop with ShopMart?

🛒 Wide Range of Products – From beauty to electronics, find everything under one roof.

🤖 Smart AI Shopping Assistant – Get instant help with queries, product suggestions, and order tracking, powered by intelligent AI.

🔒 Safe & Secure – Enjoy safe transactions and worry-free shopping.

🚚 Fast Delivery – Get your products delivered quickly and reliably.

ShopMart isn’t just an online store – it’s your trusted shopping companion designed to make every purchase simple, smart, and satisfying.

## How to run:
- Inside the Index.HTML in Vs Code , Click on Go Live and the web page will load , in the bottom right corner click on the Blue-Coloured Box Saying "Chat With Us 👋". There you can chat with our AI ChatBot named Lyro AI , which will resolve all your queries/Issues related to the Shopping Website.
